Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. Follow the steps below to request deletion of your Numerology Gateway account and all associated data.

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account using one of the following methods:

Method 1: Delete from the App

  1. 1 Open the Numerology Gateway app on your device
  2. 2 Navigate to Settings (bottom tab bar)
  3. 3 Scroll down to the Account section
  4. 4 Tap "Delete Account"
  5. 5 Confirm the deletion when prompted. Your account and data will be permanently removed.

Method 2: Request via Email

If you are unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by sending an email to:

Account Deletion Request

privacy@numerologygateway.com

Please include the email address associated with your account in your request.

Data That Will Be Deleted

Upon account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed within 30 days:

Data Type Action Timeframe
Account profile (name, email) Deleted Within 30 days
Numerology analysis results Deleted Within 30 days
Development plans and progress Deleted Within 30 days
Task notes and reflections Deleted Within 30 days
Subscription history Deleted Within 30 days
Login credentials Deleted Immediately
Anonymized analytics data Retained Up to 12 months
Legal/compliance records Retained As required by law

Important Notice

Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once your account is deleted, all your numerology analyses, development plans, task progress, and personal data cannot be recovered. If you have an active subscription, please cancel it through your app store (Google Play or Apple App Store) before deleting your account to avoid future charges.

Data Retention Period

After you request account deletion:

  • • Your account will be deactivated immediately and you will be signed out
  • • All personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days
  • • Anonymized, non-identifiable analytics data may be retained for up to 12 months for service improvement
  • • Records required by law (e.g., financial transactions) will be retained as legally mandated
