Welcome to Numerology Gateway. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information You Provide

  • Full name (for numerology calculations)

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (type, operating system, unique device identifiers)

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide numerology analysis and personalized insights

  • Improve and optimize our application
  • Communicate with you about updates and features
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Artificial Intelligence (AI) Usage

Numerology Gateway uses artificial intelligence to enhance your experience. Here's how AI is used in our application:

4.1 AI-Powered Features

  • Personalized numerology interpretations based on your profile
  • Dynamic development plans tailored to your numerology results
  • Intelligent recommendations for personal growth
  • Dashboard customization based on your usage patterns

4.2 AI Decision Transparency

Our AI systems analyze your numerology data (name and birth date) to generate insights. The AI does not make autonomous decisions that affect your legal rights. All AI-generated content is clearly labeled, and you maintain full control over your data.

4.3 Data Used by AI

The AI processes only the data you explicitly provide (name and birth date) along with your interaction history within the app. We do not use external data sources to profile you.

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS/SSL)

  • Access controls and authentication requirements
  • Secure cloud infrastructure with SOC 2 compliance

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including to satisfy legal, accounting, or reporting requirements.

For registered users, we retain account data until you request deletion. For anonymous usage, data is retained for up to 12 months for analytics purposes.
